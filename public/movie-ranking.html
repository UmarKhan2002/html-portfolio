The Best Movies According to Umar

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A powerful and uplifting story about hope, resilience, and the human spirit in the face of adversity.

The Godfather (1972)

A sprawling and epic saga of an Italian-American Mafia family, lauded for its complex characters, superb acting, and realistic portrayal of organized crime.

Citizen Kane (1941)

A groundbreaking film that revolutionized storytelling techniques and cinematography, still admired for its innovative use of light, shadow, deep focus shots, and narrative structure.
